NXT Iron Survivor Challenge Qualifying Matches Confirmed For 11/14 WWE NXT

July 21, 2024
blog

The next two qualifying bouts for the Iron Survivor Challenge matches at WWE NXT Deadline have been confirmed.

As previously announced, WWE Hall of Famers Mick Foley, JBL, Lita, and Jerry Lawler will pick the qualifying matches, which will be held throughout November on NXT. Dijak and Tiffany Stratton won qualifying bouts on the November 7 episode of WWE NXT.

WWE Hall of Famer Lita unveiled her picks for the participants on social media. She announced that Roxanne Perez will face Lash Legend on the November 14 episode of NXT, and Trick Williams will take on Joe Coffey. The winners will advance to the Iron Survivor Challenge matches at NXT Deadline.

Roxanne Perez won the Women’s Iron Survivor Challenge match at NXT Deadline 2022 and went on to win the NXT Women’s Championship.

Updated Card For 11/14 WWE NXT

The updated card for Tuesday’s episode of WWE NXT is below.

  • : Chase U (Duke Hudson & Andre Chase) (c) vs. The D’Angelo Family (Tony D’Angelo & Channing “Stacks” Lorenzo)
  • Supernova Sessions with Alpha Academy
  • : Roxanne Perez vs. Lash Legend
  • : Trick Williams vs. Joe Coffey

WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs on November 14.
