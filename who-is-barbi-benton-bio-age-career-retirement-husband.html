Barbi Benton born on 28 January 1950 is an American retired model, actress, songwriter, television personality, and singer. She is best known for her frequent appearance in the Playboy magazine and also for her role in the comedy television show “Hee Haw” and also for recording several successful albums in the 1970s. Barbi left the scene to raise her family after the birth of her firstborn in 1986.

Barbi Benton Early Life and Career Facts

At the age of 16, Barbi was already a model and was featured several times on Playboy magazine. She posed nude for the Playboy magazine’s March 1970 issue, December 1973, January 1875, and December 1985 editions.Barbi then switched her focus to film acting. She starred in the comedy sitcom “Sugar Time”. After some shows, she moved into the music industry. Her debut album “Brass Buckles” entered the Billboard’s Country singles chart.

She continued to drop several albums. For example, she released an album in 1976 called “Something New”. She also released another album in 1978 called “Ain’t That Just the Way”. Some of the singles in the album include “Brass Buckles”, “Movie Magazine, Stars in Her Eyes”, “Roll You Like a Wheel”, “The Reverend Bob” and “Staying Power”. Her last and final album was also released in 1979.
